Terms of Service

Strathmore Marketplace ("FleaStrath") is a peer-to-peer platform for Strathmore University students and staff to buy, sell, and exchange goods within the campus community. By creating an account you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information during registration and keep your account details up to date.
  • Only list items you are legally entitled to sell, with an honest description of their condition.
  • Conduct all trades respectfully and in good faith with fellow students.
  • Follow any campus safety guidance when arranging to meet a buyer or seller in person.

Strathmore Marketplace facilitates connections between buyers and sellers but is not a party to any transaction between users, and does not process payments on their behalf.

Privacy Policy

We collect only what's needed to run the marketplace: your full name, institutional email, phone number, and username at registration, plus whatever you choose to list for sale. Your password is never stored in plain text — it is hashed by our authentication provider.

  • Your name and shop name (if you're a vendor) are visible to other logged-in users so they can identify who they're buying from.
  • Your email is only shared with a buyer if you list a product and they choose to contact you about it.
  • Administrators can view account and listing details to moderate the platform and verify vendor accounts.

We do not sell or share your data with third parties outside of the university marketplace context.

Help Center

How do I become a verified vendor?

Register with the Vendor option selected. An administrator reviews and approves new vendor accounts before your "Verified" badge appears.

I didn't receive my verification code.

Check your spam folder first. If it still hasn't arrived after a few minutes, use the Resend Code link on the verification page.

How do I contact a seller?

Open any listing and use the Contact Vendor button — it opens an email addressed directly to the seller.

Something's not working — who do I ask?

Email admin-support@strathmore.edu and an administrator will help.
